## Technical / Engineering Framing

- [Strong past-tense verb] [what you built or automated] using [tools/languages],
  resulting in [concrete outcome — time saved, reliability gained, coverage improved].

- [Strong past-tense verb] [system, pipeline, or integration], enabling [team or org]
  to [do something they could not do before or do significantly faster].

- [Strong past-tense verb] [process, standard, or framework], reducing [pain point]
  for [number or type of teams/users].

Examples of strong verbs: Built, Automated, Reduced, Eliminated, Designed, Engineered,
Integrated, Deployed, Migrated, Refactored, Established, Piloted, Implemented.

## Cross-Functional / Collaboration Framing

- Partnered with [team A] and [team B] to [shared objective], translating stakeholder
  requirements into [deliverable — a process, system, or policy].

- Acted as primary liaison between [team A] and [team B], ensuring [outcome —
  alignment, consistent data, faster handoffs, etc.].

- Gathered requirements from [number] teams and translated them into [design/spec/API
  standard], increasing adoption and reducing friction.

## Leadership / Impact Framing

- Led a [duration] initiative to [high-level goal], collaborating with [number or
  type of stakeholders] and delivering [outcome].

- Owned end-to-end delivery of [project or system], from requirements gathering
  through deployment and stakeholder sign-off.

- Drove adoption of [practice, tool, or process] across [team/org], resulting in
  [measurable or qualitative improvement].
