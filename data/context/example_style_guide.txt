Tone: professional but human — no jargon, no filler, no corporate speak.

Numbers: qualitative impact only unless exact figures exist in the source resume.

Section order: Contact → Summary (only if it adds genuine value, otherwise omit) →
Technical Skills → Professional Experience → Education.

Headings: bold and underlined for section headers; consistent with base resume layout.

Sentences: short. Break anything over 25 words into two sentences.

Tense: past tense for previous roles; present tense for current role only.

Formatting: no tables, no columns in experience bullets. Plain readable text.

Summary section (if included): 2–3 sentences max. No "results-driven professional"
or similar openers. Start with your specialty and what you bring.
